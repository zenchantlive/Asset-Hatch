## Design Tips

### Color Scheme (until you get Asset Hatch brand colors):
- Primary: #2E7D32 (Professional green)
- Secondary: #1976D2 (Professional blue)  
- Accent: #F57C00 (Warm orange)
- Text: #263238 (Dark blue-gray)

### Typography:
- Title font: Roboto Bold, 32-44pt
- Subtitle font: Roboto Medium, 24-28pt
- Body font: Roboto Regular, 18-24pt

### Visual Elements:
- Use consistent icons throughout
- Add timeline graphics for journey slides
- Include professional photos where indicated
- Use charts/graphs for data visualization
- Maintain consistent spacing and alignment

### Best Practices:
- Keep text minimal (maximum 6 lines per slide)
- Use high-quality images
- Test readability from a distance
- Practice timing (aim for 45-60 seconds per slide)
- Prepare speaker notes for additional context
