- What Asset Hatch is (context for audience)

- Initial discovery of Asset Hatch
- What attracted you to join/participate

### 5. Growth & Development
- Major milestones achieved

- Key projects or initiatives

### 6. Key Accomplishments
- Quantifiable achievements

### 7. Relationships & Network
- Key mentors and supporters

### 8. Challenges Overcome

### 9. Transformation & Impact

### 10. Future Vision
- How Asset Hatch shaped your future

### 12. Contact & Thank You

## Design Considerations

### Branding Elements
- Use Asset Hatch's brand colors (to be confirmed)
- Incorporate company logo consistently
- Use approved typography
- Maintain professional, modern aesthetic

### Visual Elements
- Timeline graphics
- Progress indicators
- Milestone markers
- Professional photos of key moments
- Infographics for data/achievements
- Consistent slide templates

### Storytelling Approach
- Start with a compelling hook
- Use narrative arc: challenge → struggle → growth → success
- Include personal anecdotes
- Show, don't just tell (specific examples)
- Emotional connection through authenticity

## Content Guidelines

### What to Include
- Specific metrics and data points
- Real examples and stories
- Photos and visuals
- Quotes from mentors/colleagues
- Timeline of key events

### What to Avoid
- Generic statements without substance
- Too much text on slides
- Inconsistent formatting
- Outdated or low-quality images
- Overly technical jargon

## Technical Specifications
- 16:9 aspect ratio
- Maximum 20 slides
- Consistent slide templates
- Readable fonts (minimum 24pt)
- High-quality images
- Smooth transitions between sections
